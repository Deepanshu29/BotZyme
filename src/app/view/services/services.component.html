Augmented Reality

Augmented reality (AR) adds digital elements to a live view often by using the camera on a smartphone.

Virtual Reality

Virtual reality (VR) implies a complete immersion experience that shuts out the physical world.

Mixed Reality

MR is the merging of real and virtual worlds to produce new environments.



Internet of Things

The internet of things, or IoT, is a system of interrelated computing devices, mechanical and digital machines.

Machine Learning

ML is an application of AI that provides systems the ability to automatically learn from experience without being explicitly programmed.
